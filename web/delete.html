DELETE MY DATA

Murge

We respect your right to control your data. If you'd like us to delete your Murge account and all associated data, you can request it below.

WHAT GETS DELETED

  • Your player profile (display name, streaks)
  • All daily scores and leaderboard entries
  • Your authentication account (if connected)
  • Any email address associated with your account

WHAT STAYS

Anonymous analytics data (collected by GameAnalytics) is not linked to your identity and cannot be individually deleted.

HOW TO REQUEST

Send an email to the address below with your display name (shown in Settings) or the email address linked to your account. We'll process your request within 7 days.

REQUEST DELETION

You can also delete the app from your device to remove all locally stored data (scores, preferences, auth tokens). Server-side data requires a deletion request.
