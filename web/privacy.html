PRIVACY POLICY

Murge

Murge is a daily merge puzzle game. We take your privacy seriously and collect only what's needed to make the game work. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

WHAT WE COLLECT

Account information — You can play anonymously or connect an Apple or Google account. If you connect an account, your provider may share your name and email address. We only use this to link your scores across devices. Anonymous players are identified by a random ID generated on the device.

Display name — A name you choose (or one we generate for you). Shown on the leaderboard. You can change it anytime in Settings.

Gameplay data — Your daily scores, merge counts, chain length, and streak. This powers the leaderboard and your personal stats.

Analytics events — We use GameAnalytics to understand how the game is played (e.g., how often games are started, average scores, whether features like shake are used). This data is anonymous and not linked to your identity.

WHAT WE DON'T COLLECT

  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, or files
  • No advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • No cross-app tracking

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • Display your score on the daily leaderboard
  • Track your play streak
  • Show your rank relative to other players
  • Link your scores across devices (if you connect an account)
  • Understand aggregate gameplay patterns to improve the game

AUTHENTICATION

Murge uses Supabase Auth for account management. You can play without an account (anonymous mode). If you choose to connect an Apple or Google account, we use their sign-in services to verify your identity. Your provider may share your name and email address. We only use this to link your scores across devices.

You can sign out at any time in Settings. Signing out creates a new anonymous session. You can sign back in to recover your linked scores.

WHERE DATA IS STORED

Gameplay data (scores, names, streaks) and authentication are managed by Supabase, a cloud database service. Analytics data is processed by GameAnalytics. Both services store data securely and are hosted in the United States and European Union.

DATA RETENTION

Your scores and leaderboard data are kept indefinitely as part of the game's history. If you'd like your data removed, contact us and we'll delete it.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

  • Supabase — Database, authentication, and serverless functions (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Apple Sign In — Account authentication on iOS (apple.com/legal/privacy)
  • Google Sign In — Account authentication (policies.google.com/privacy)
  • GameAnalytics — Anonymous gameplay analytics (gameanalytics.com/privacy)

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Murge does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require an account and collects minimal data.

YOUR CHOICES

  • You can play without connecting an account
  • You can connect or disconnect an Apple or Google account in Settings
  • You can change your display name anytime in Settings
  • You can turn off sound effects and haptics in Settings
  • You can delete the app to remove all local data from your device
  • To request deletion of server-side data, visit our data deletion page
